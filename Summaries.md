# Journal of Market Research 
- **Online Venting: The Impact of Temporal Proximity Cues and Emotionality on Perceptions of Negative Online Review Value**: Consumers identify a negative review that combines near temporal proximity (i.e., posted or written in a way that makes the experience feel temporally close) with negative emotionality as a form of “venting.” They then ascribe the information in the negative review to reviewer-related attributes rather than to relevant product or service quality attributes.
